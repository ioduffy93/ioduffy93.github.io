Overview

Iceland is one of Britain’s fastest-growing and most innovative retailers. Winning Britain’s top online retailer for the third year in succession. While also stirring up the landscape by pledging to cut palm oil from their products and removing all plastic packaging by 2023.

Since changing their strategy in 2015, Iceland has sought to improve public perceptions of frozen food by underlining the real advantages only it can offer to consumers. This change in strategy has bore fruit as Last year, Iceland recorded “ahead of the market” growth, with sales up 8.8% while their brand tracking shows significant increases in key metrics- Quality, Trust and Recommendation. However, despite now eating into the “Big 4’s” market share; key competitors Aldi and Lidl are still winning over customers- Aldi recently winning supermarket of the year.

Therefore, there is a need to improve consumer conversion- increase consideration. In order to address this, I am suggesting we conduct a piece of bespoke research to get a fresh understanding of who Iceland’s current and potential consumers are.
