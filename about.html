Stage 2: Brand Perceptions

To understand consumer perceptions of Iceland and aid brand planning

Why?

To quantify consumers needs and priorities, while, measuring perceptions of Iceland vs. competitors

Who?

We will recruit our audience using our trusted panel provider to conduct the fieldwork, a sample of N:600 respondents

A sample of N: 600

All are either the main/share the household food shop

Spread equally across gender and age brackets (from 18+)

Will also aim for minimum n:100 aware of Iceland

An audience size of n=600, is recommended so that during analysis we are able to split the data into different groups (e.g.  age, interest – linked to brand preference etc.) and provide a statistically robust base.

What?

Up to 20 minute online survey, covering grocery category perceptions/experiences and brand perceptions: :

  • Screening/Demographics

  • Audience Interests & Lifestyle Behaviours

  • Buying Habits & Behaviour

  • Brand & Ad Awareness of Grocery Brands (spontaneous & prompted – Iceland and competitors)

  • Favorability, Opinion and Perceptions of the Grocery Market- association, likeability (Iceland and competitors)  

  • Consideration and Purchase Intent (Iceland and competitors)  

  • Introduction to Iceland brand Imagery/ads to ascertain perceptions (showcase brand info, comms and gain feedback)  

  • Statement & Associations Specific to Iceland (for example; relevant to me, good value, good quality etc.)

  • Iceland Consumer Understanding (who do they think Iceland is aimed at etc.)
