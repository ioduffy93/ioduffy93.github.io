Research Objectives & Approach

Research Objectives

• To better understand Iceland consumers and improve targeting - to introduce the brand to potential consumers and understand the current brand appeal – who has their interest piqued

• To understand consumer needs, attitudes and behaviours within this category

• To understand and measure the strength of Iceland's brand perceptions amongst its target audience vs. competitors / relevant brands- what are the key barriers and opportunities.

• To quantify the grocery shopping decision making process and explore current brand choice reasons

• To explore how to make consumers feel closer to the Iceland brand; how to improve consideration and strengthen brand perceptions.

• To test the latest brand creative and communications (messaging, appeal and impact on brand perceptions)

Research Approach

For this project I have suggested using a dual methodology: qualitative blogs and quantitative testing. I am recommending this multidiscipline approach as it delivers stronger, more credible results that get to the heart of the consumer.

Online Blogs

The qualitative research enables us to delve into, and explore, consumers’ psyches to understand their needs and attitudes and therefore the opportunities for Iceland.

Online Survey

We will then be able to quantify the size of these opportunities through an online survey and gain greater insight into competitors and the decision making process.

Actionable Insights

The goal of this research is provide bespoke insights, which will fuel and inspire brand strategy and ensure it is grounded in consumer knowledge.
